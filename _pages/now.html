Work

I'm working as Design Consultant on various projects for start ups, and continuing to develop my own game ideas.

  1. 1. Designing Gamification, Learning Games and Playful Interactions for Crejo.fun. It's an online learning platform for holistic development of children through extracurricular activities.

  2. 2. Working with a Stealth Startup in Designing Gamification Strategies for a new product designed for young professionals dealing with mental health and wellbeing related issues.

  3. 3. Helping another startup LECODE GAMES with product design, strategy, UX, and game design for different products they are developing in the area of serious games and learners communities.

  4. 4 Working on my new tabletop game - Guldasta(working title), inspired by an Indian ritual of offering flowers to the gods in the temple to get their blessings. This project started as a part of The Tabletop Mentorship Program with Jeff, and now is in the second phase of playtesting. The latest iteration is up on TTS and I'm looking forward to get back to the momentum with the playtests. You can see a rough cut of the gameplay video and a sell sheet. Some initial logs that I missed to post further at lonely night project.


Living

I left home two months back in July to Shillong, Meghalaya where again I couldn't go anywhere as the lockdown continued in the state. Now I'm at my brother's place in Pilani for a while. In the coming weeks, I'm going to Himachal. I'm preparing a toy making workshop for children at the Udaan Learning Center near Palampur. I'll be staying in Himachal for a month or so at different places and then come back home for Diwali.
