I'm living with my parents in my hometown. I rarely step out of my house, sometimes my room. My schedule changes everyday and shift by two to four hours, yesterday I slept at 5am and today it's already 6am, so I will go to sleep after my morning walk and breakfast.

Update : Due to my bad schedule and food habits, my body has developed a Non-alcoholic steatohepatitis (NASH), a kind of fatty liver disease. Doctor has suggested to drastically change my schedule, diet, and do regular exercise, that means I've to maintain my body more consciously, and I can't just keep on living the way I used to (typical 30's problems). So, from now on, I'm trying to live more bodily and less in mind.

I will keep updating this page as my interest, projects, and life changes. Last update - 25th Oct 2020.
