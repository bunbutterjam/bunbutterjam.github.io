Hi, I'm Swapnesh, I design games, play and learning experiences. I work as an independent game designer and researcher, with studios, startups and in collaboration with other designers, artists, researchers, and developers.

Currently, I'm working with Grok! building 21st century skills for kids using games. Previously I've worked with Crejo, BYJU's, Bright Champ, Vihara, IITGN, NID, and other organizations in the area of game design, research, gamification, and playful learning.

In my spare time, I volunteer with social organizations working in education, in conducting play workshops for children, and developing playful learning tools and materials with teachers and homeschoolers.
