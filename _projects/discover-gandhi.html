Discover Gandhi is an online open platform launched by NCERT on the 150th Birth Anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi. It is a curated collection of interesting information, articles and multi-media exhibition on the life and workd of Mahatma Gandhi. The platform provides a space for students, teachers, educators, researchers, and historians to explore the life, and teachings of Gandhi, his philosophy, principles, and values in a contemporary context through different activities like competitions, quizzes, and discussions.

I worked as a User Experience Designer on the project for MASSPL, who developed the website. My role was to design the information architecture, interactions and the interface.
